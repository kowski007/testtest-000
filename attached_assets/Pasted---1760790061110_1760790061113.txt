╚═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

    🎯 INPUT: Verified content/project metadata
    ⚙️  PROCESS:
        • One wallet signature (Zora factory)
        • Deploy as tradeable coin on Base
        • Store metadata on IPFS (permanent)
        • Record to database instantly
        • Background batch sync to blockchain
    📤 OUTPUT: Live tradeable coin with contract address

    WHY THIS MATTERS FOR PUBLIC GOODS:
    
    ✅ On-chain proof of work          ✅ Verifiable impact metrics
    ✅ Grant application evidence      ✅ Community support visible
    ✅ Retroactive funding potential   ✅ Trading creates liquidity
